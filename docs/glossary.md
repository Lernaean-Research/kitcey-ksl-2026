# Glossary

- KSL: Kit-State Lifecycle.
- KSP: Kitcey Synergy Principle.
- CV: coefficient of variation, std divided by mean.
- Calibration window: post-crossing portion used for in-sample row checks.
- Held-out window: final trajectory segment used for out-of-sample checks.
- UV gate: composite fail logic used by the scorecard decision layer.
- SPARC: Spitzer Photometry and Accurate Rotation Curves galaxy dataset.
